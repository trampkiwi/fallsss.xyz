fall(sss) exists across a designated physical location and online at www.fallsss.xyz.
It uses the time of sunrise and sunset specific to the location to generate a space where movements of decrease and decline are explored on the backdrop of algorithmically coded colourfield.

Past: Hirvitalo - Pispalan nykytaiteen keskus (Center of Contemporary Art Pispala), Tampere, Finland, 13 July - 1 August 2019

Present: London, UK, 27 March 2019 -



The time of sunrise, sunset, etc. is imported from timeanddate.com, and javascript libraries HSLuv, Howler.js, seedrandom.js were used.
